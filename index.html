Who Are We?

Happy Gas Improv Troupe is Kentucky’s longest running improvisational comedy troupe.

Founded in 1999 by WKU students Colin Thornton and Josh Chapman, Happy Gas has grown for decades entirely under student control. Performing both shortform and longform improv, Happy Gas operates under its motto “The root of comedy is trust.”
